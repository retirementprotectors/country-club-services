Who is Brooks?

A young entrepreneur
built on trust.

Brooks Millang started Country Club Services the old-fashioned way — knocking on doors, summer 2025, with nothing but a handshake offer and a willingness to work. No app. No ads. Just a young entrepreneur who figured out that the best way to build a business is to earn it one neighbor at a time.

Every neighbor on the list chose Brooks because he showed up, delivered, and came back. That same reliability is what drives every window wash, every lawn, and every detail job. He takes pride in the work — not because he has to, but because that's how you build something worth building.

"Not happy with the job? Brooks will make it right — that's the guarantee."
